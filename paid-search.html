Three Mistakes Killing Your Google Ads

Most Google Ads accounts are bleeding money in ways that look profitable on paper. Strong ROAS, but no real growth. High spend, but shrinking margins.

We've audited hundreds of accounts and the same patterns emerge again and again. Fix these three mistakes and everything changes.

  • Branded search eating 80%+ of budget while looking efficient
  • Performance Max with no guardrails cannibalizing other campaigns
  • No visibility into true incremental new customer acquisition
  • Conversion tracking firing on the wrong events
  • Shopping feeds with missing attributes and poor structure
01

Over Spending on Branded

Your branded ROAS looks incredible—but you're paying for customers who would have converted anyway. That's not growth, it's cannibalization.

02

Under Spending on Non-Branded

The real opportunity is in category and competitor terms, high intent searchers who don't know you yet. That's where incremental growth lives.

03

Bottom Funnel Only

If you're only capturing demand at the bottom of the funnel, you're fighting for scraps. Real scale requires creating demand upstream.

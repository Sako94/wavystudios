The Math

Revenue Comes From Four Places

When you optimize all four levers together, the gains multiply instead of add.

Small Wins Compound Big

Most brands optimize one thing at a time. We optimize everything in parallel. A 15% improvement in conversion rate, plus a 20% lift in AOV, plus a 25% increase in repeat purchase rate doesn't give you 60% more revenue—it gives you 73%.

The multiplier effect: Paid media brings customers in. Creative determines cost of acquisition. CRO maximizes revenue per visitor. Retention compounds it all over time. Each lever amplifies the others.

This is why integrated systems beat isolated campaigns. When your retention team knows what your creative team is testing, and your CRO team knows what your paid team is learning—the whole system gets smarter together.

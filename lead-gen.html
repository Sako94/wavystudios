Defining Quality

What We Mean by Lead Quality

Lead quality isn't a feeling. It's measurable.

Fit
Does this lead match your ideal customer profile?
Intent
Are they actively looking for a solution or just browsing?
Timing
Are they ready to buy now or in six months?
Engagement
Did they just fill out a form or spend 10 minutes on your site?

We build systems that score leads on these dimensions and optimize campaigns to generate more of the ones that actually close. That's how you scale volume without sacrificing quality.

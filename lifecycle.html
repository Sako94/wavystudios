The First to Second Order Is Everything

Here's the truth most agencies won't tell you: the battle for customer lifetime value is won or lost in the first 45 days. If a customer doesn't make a second purchase in that window, there's a 70% chance they never will.

That's why we obsess over post-purchase flows. Not just "thanks for your order" emails‚Äîbut strategic sequences that educate, cross-sell, and create the behavior patterns that turn one-time buyers into repeat customers.

The math is simple: A 10% improvement in first-to-second order conversion can increase customer LTV by 25-40%. That's the highest-leverage work in retention marketing.

We build post-purchase journeys that guide customers through product education, usage tips, complementary recommendations, and perfectly-timed replenishment reminders. Every touchpoint is designed to reduce time-to-second-order.
